# Kastel Light Node Starter

Kastel Light is the low-friction adoption surface for the adaptive organism protocol.

It lets a small business run the organism loop without first understanding ontologies, knowledge graphs, causal modelling, agentic AI or Red Rail / Blue Rail infrastructure.

## Weekly Loop

```text
check vitals
-> protect the core offer
-> spot environmental changes
-> choose one maintenance action
-> choose one learning action
-> review the result
-> bank or share what genuinely worked
```

## The Ten Starter Cards

### 1. DNA Card

- What is the core offer?
- What must not be changed casually?
- What values does it protect?
- What proof or experience supports it?
- What claim boundaries apply?

### 2. Weekly Vitals Card

- cash
- sales
- customer feedback
- main channel
- delivery quality
- operator load
- main risk
- main opportunity

### 3. Robustness Prior Card

- What could break the business?
- What dependency is most fragile?
- What needs a backup?
- What risk needs attention this week?

### 4. Scaling Prior Card

- Where could growth compound?
- What action might create a useful downstream chain?
- What route is slow but reliable?
- What route is high-upside but risky?

### 5. Adaptive Field Card

- Which field are we testing?
- Why does it matter?
- What signal are we watching?
- What would count as real learning?
- What would indicate bubble or noise?

### 6. Decision Card

- What is the decision?
- What do we believe?
- What is uncertain?
- What would teach us most?
- What is the safest next move?

### 7. Delayed Review Card

- What did we expect?
- What happened?
- Was it interpretable?
- Should we repeat, revise, stop or bank?

### 8. Script Card

- What worked?
- In what context?
- What are the limits?
- Could it transfer?
- What validation badge applies?
- What must be changed locally?

### 9. Recovery Card

- What failed or nearly failed?
- What protected us?
- What should be backed up?
- What recovery step should be documented?

### 10. Shareability Card

- Can this learning help another node?
- Can it be shared without private data?
- What context must travel with it?
- What warning should accompany it?
- What validation signal should be shown?

## Validation Badges

Light Node users should see simple validation badges:

- Observed
- Delayed checked
- Tested by N nodes
- Cross-context validated
- Banked
- Retired

The badges let a user consume commons artefacts without inspecting a full evidence trail every time.

## Minimum Export

Every Light Node should be able to export:

- DNA card
- weekly reviews
- open Action Intents
- open Clarify Intents
- delayed reviews
- script bank
- recovery checklist
- shareability decisions

Exportability is part of the anti-capture posture. The app should never become the only nervous system of the business.
